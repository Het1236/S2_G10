
Task: Generate a lecture scribe.

Purpose:
The output must be a faithful, exam-ready lecture scribe that reconstructs exactly what was taught in the lecture using only the provided context. The scribe must function as reference material for preparing for a reading-based / closed-notes exam.

Constraints:

Use only the attached context (lecture slides/PDF and relevant textbook excerpts).

Do not invent, extend, simplify, reinterpret, or add intuition.

Follow the lecture’s structure and scope strictly.

Prioritize correctness, precise notation, and explicit reasoning.

Include definitions, notation, assumptions, theorem/result statements, proofs or proof sketches, and worked examples with intermediate steps whenever present in the context.

Maintain clear logical flow between ideas.

Write in formal, neutral academic tone.

Do not include commentary, motivation, or personal explanations.
